Currency,Government,and Society

Germans use the Euro as currency. 1 euro is 1.119 U.S. dollars. The German currency before the euro was the Deutsche mark or German mark. One German mark is equal to 61 U.S. cents.

The German government type is Federal Republic. A federal republic is a federation of states with a republican form of government.

In the the German society, people are mostly friendly and kids do well in school/adults in work.
